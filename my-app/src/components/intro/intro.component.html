BMW F11 550i Touring LCI – Registry & Archive Documenting each known individual example of the BMW Touring F11 550i LCI.

This website is dedicated to one of the rarest modern BMWs ever produced: the BMW F11 550i Touring LCI equipped with the N63B44TÜ engine. Based on information I received from a trusted source, only around 222 left-hand drive and 25 right-hand drive cars were built — making this model a true hidden gem in BMW’s history.

If you own one, know of one, or can contribute data about an individual car, this registry aims to collect and preserve the history of every known example. Entries include (when available): chassis number, build date, specification, ownership history, location, and supporting photos.

Have more information to share? Please contact me at nancologist@gmail.com.
